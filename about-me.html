About me

My name is Aidan. I also go by Churro or Cinnamon. I don't mind which of these you choose to refer to me as. I am agender, I use any pronouns.
I am 16 years old. I consider myself a decent artist. I am not the best, nor will I be to everyone, as that is subjective. I try to improve my art and am willing to try new things. When you commission me, I will try to do something new.
I live in Tucson Arizona; my timezone is MST (UTC-7).
If you want to know more about me, you know how to contact me.
